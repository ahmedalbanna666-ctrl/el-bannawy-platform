# GLOSSARY.md

# El-bannawy Platform
## Project Glossary

Version: 1.0

---

# Purpose

This document defines the official terminology used throughout the El-bannawy Platform.

These definitions are mandatory.

AI Agents and developers MUST use these names exactly as documented.

---

## Lesson

A Lesson is the smallest learning unit.

A Lesson is NOT a video.

A Lesson consists of:

- Lesson Videos (one or more, each with its own timeline and activities)
- Activities (dynamically generated from uploaded Word document, per video)
- Homework (optional)
- End Lesson Assessment (optional)

---

## Unit

A Unit is a collection of Lessons.

Students complete Lessons to complete a Unit.

---

## Story

A Story is a curriculum section independent from Units.

It has its own Lessons, Videos, Homework and Quizzes.

---

## Interactive Video

A recorded lesson video containing Interactive Timeline Events.

A lesson may contain multiple Interactive Videos, each with its own timeline events and activities.

When playback reaches a timeline event, the video pauses and the configured activity opens.

Students cannot bypass timeline events.

Timeline events and activities belong to the video — not the lesson.

---

## Activity

A learning exercise generated from the uploaded Word document.

Activities belong to a specific lesson video — not to the lesson.

Activities are rendered dynamically below the corresponding lesson video.

Supported types include Vocabulary, Multiple Choice, True / False, Matching, Fill in the Blanks, Drag & Drop, Reading, Conversation, Speaking, Writing, Paragraph, and more.

---

## Vocabulary

The vocabulary section generated from the uploaded Word document.

Contains pronunciation, meaning and examples.

---

## Homework

Student practice completed after studying a Lesson.

Homework is optional and is enabled or disabled by the teacher.

Homework is graded automatically when applicable.

---

## End Lesson Assessment

The optional final assessment at the end of a Lesson.

When enabled and required by the teacher, passing it may be required for lesson completion and unlocking the next lesson.

---

## Final Review

A review module opened only by teachers during revision periods.

---

## Learn From Mistakes

A personalized collection of every incorrectly answered question.

Students can revisit and solve them again.

---

## Ask El-bannawy AI

The official AI assistant inside the platform.

Answers questions using approved educational content.

---

## Interactive Timeline Event

A configured point in the lesson video that triggers an activity.

Timeline events may be Required or Optional.

When the video reaches a timeline event, playback pauses and the activity opens.

---

## Required Activity

An activity that the student must complete before the video resumes.

Required activities cannot be bypassed.

---

## Optional Activity

An activity that the student may skip.

The video resumes when the student dismisses the optional activity.

---

## Communication & Notification Engine

The centralized platform component responsible for all communication between the platform and students.

Supports multiple delivery channels including Mobile Push and In-App Notifications.

Future channels include Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and Browser Push.

---

## Live Class

A scheduled online lesson between teacher and students.

---

## XP

Experience Points.

Used only for ranking and progress.

XP cannot be purchased.

---

## Coins

Premium virtual currency.

Used to purchase lessons, units or courses.

Coins do not affect rankings.

---

## Referral

The student invitation system.

Rewards are granted according to documented business rules.

---

## Dashboard

The main landing page after login.

Displays personalized student information.

---

## Student

A learner enrolled in the platform.

---

## Teacher

Creates educational content and manages students.

---

## Secretary

Manages registrations, subscriptions and operational tasks.

---

## Administrator

Has full platform permissions.

---

## Support

Handles technical issues and user requests.

---

## Business Rule

A rule that defines platform behavior.

Business Rules must never be changed without approval.

---

This glossary is the official terminology reference for the entire project.
