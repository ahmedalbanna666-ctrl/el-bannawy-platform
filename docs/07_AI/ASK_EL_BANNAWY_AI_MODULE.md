# El-bannawy Platform
## Ask El-bannawy AI Module Requirements

Version: 1.0.0

---

# Purpose

Ask El-bannawy AI is the official AI learning assistant inside the El-bannawy Platform.

Unlike general AI chatbots, this assistant is designed specifically for English education and is restricted to the educational content approved by El-bannawy.

Its primary objective is to help students learn—not simply provide answers.

---

# Objectives

The AI Assistant must:

- Explain lessons.
- Explain grammar.
- Explain vocabulary.
- Help solve exercises.
- Analyze student mistakes.
- Recommend revision content.
- Personalize learning.
- Encourage independent thinking.

# AI Restrictions

The AI MUST NOT:

- Generate inappropriate content.
- Ignore platform curriculum.
- Invent answers.
- Reveal hidden exam answers.
- Modify student grades.
- Modify XP.
- Modify Coins.

# Lesson Awareness

When the student opens AI from a lesson,

the AI automatically receives:

- Lesson ID
- Unit ID
- Grade
- Stage
