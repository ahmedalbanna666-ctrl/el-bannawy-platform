# Lesson Page Structure

Video 1 (with Interactive Timeline + Activities)

↓

Video 2 (with Interactive Timeline + Activities)

↓

Video N (with Interactive Timeline + Activities)

↓

Homework Section (if enabled by teacher)

↓

End Lesson Assessment (if enabled by teacher)

Videos may be sequential (locked until previous completes) or any-order.

---

# Interactive Timeline Experience

Student watches video.

↓

Playback reaches a Timeline Event.

↓

Video pauses automatically.

↓

Activity card opens inline or as a modal.

↓

Student completes the activity.

↓

Student cannot skip or bypass.

↓

Video resumes from the same position.

↓

Next Timeline Event (if configured).

# Notification Preferences

Students can manage:

- Lesson Reminders
- Homework Reminders
- Live Session Reminders
- Achievement Notifications
- Motivational Messages
- Study Tips
- Teacher Announcements

Toggle switches for each preference.

Critical notifications cannot be disabled.
