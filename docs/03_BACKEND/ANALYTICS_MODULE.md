# El-bannawy Platform
## Analytics Module Requirements

Version: 1.0.0

---

# Purpose

The Analytics Module is the Business Intelligence (BI) engine of the El-bannawy Platform.

It collects, processes and visualizes educational, operational and financial data to help administrators, teachers and decision-makers improve the learning experience.

Analytics are not reports.

Reports describe what happened.

Analytics explain why it happened and predict what should happen next.

---

# Objectives

The Analytics Module must:

- Measure platform performance.
- Measure student engagement.
- Measure learning effectiveness.
- Detect weak educational areas.
- Support business decisions.
- Predict future trends.
- Improve teaching quality.
